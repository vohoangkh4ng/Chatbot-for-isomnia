# Title: Association between Beverage Consumption and Sleep Quality in Adolescents

## Summary: This study investigates the relationship between various beverage consumption, including water, 100% pure fruit juice, and sugar-sweetened beverages (SSBs), and sleep quality among adolescents aged 14 to 17 in Qu√©bec, Canada. The research involved 218 participants and utilized validated questionnaires to assess beverage intake and sleep quality. Findings indicate that the consumption of energy drinks and sugar-sweetened coffee negatively impacts sleep quality, with notable differences between genders. The study suggests targeted public health interventions to reduce energy drink consumption in boys and sugar-sweetened coffee intake in girls to enhance sleep quality.
